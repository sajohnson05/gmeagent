# Affiliation Agreement Lapse Workflow

## Trigger
- Agreement expiration within 90 days
- OR expired agreement

---

## Workflow

1. Detection
- Program Operations or Accreditation identifies lapse risk

2. Notification
- Accreditation and Finance notified

3. Impact Assessment
- Identify affected programs and rotations

4. Threshold Check
- If expired → immediate escalation to Director of GME
- If high-risk → escalate to Executive

5. Resolution
- Renew agreement OR adjust rotations

6. Compliance Review
- Ensure ACGME compliance

7. Documentation
- Update agreement records

---

## Ownership

- Program Operations: tracking
- Accreditation: compliance oversight
- Finance: agreement terms
- Director of GME: escalation
