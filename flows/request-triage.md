# Request Triage Flow

## Purpose
Defines how incoming requests are evaluated and routed.

---

## Step 1: Identify Request Type

- Strategic
- Operational
- Financial
- Accreditation
- Resident-related
- Faculty-related
- Data/reporting

---

## Step 2: Determine Scope

- Program-level
- Division-level
- Institutional
- Cross-functional

---

## Step 3: Assign Agents

Route to appropriate agents based on type and scope.

---

## Step 4: Sequence Work

- Upstream analysis first
- Then dependent functions

---

## Step 5: Consolidate Output

- Remove duplication
- Ensure ownership
- Provide final structured response

---

## Step 6: Validate

- Is the output complete?
- Is ownership clear?
- Are risks identified?

---

## Design Standard

All work must go through structured triage before execution.
