# Interaction Map

## Purpose
Defines how the major functions of the GME office interact in practice.

## Executive Layer
- DIO ↔ Associate DIO ↔ Director of GME

## Administrative Leadership
- Director of GME ↔ All division leaders

## Functional Interaction Map
- Accreditation & Compliance ↔ Data & Analytics ↔ Faculty Development
- Resident Affairs ↔ Program Directors ↔ HR / Legal
- Finance & Medicare ↔ Strategy & Growth ↔ CFO / executive finance
- Data & Analytics ↔ All divisions
- Faculty Development ↔ Program leadership ↔ Accreditation
- Director of GME ↔ Program operations ↔ institutional functions

## Program-Level Interaction
- Program Director ↔ Associate Program Director ↔ Program Coordinator
- Program Coordinator ↔ Accreditation / Resident Affairs / Faculty Development
- Program leadership ↔ institutional office for support, escalation, and reporting

## Design Standard
No function should operate in isolation. The office should be intentionally connected so ownership is clear and information moves early.
