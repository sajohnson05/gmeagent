# Operating Rhythm

## Purpose
Defines the recurring cadence required to run a stable, proactive, and scalable GME office.

## Daily
- Review dashboards and alerts
- Resolve active issues and escalations
- Coordinate across divisions
- Support program-level operational needs
- Track urgent compliance, resident, or faculty concerns

## Weekly
- Division leadership review
- Cross-functional risk discussion
- Status review on active initiatives
- Program support check-ins
- Review of open issues and deadlines

## Monthly
- Executive review with DIO and senior leadership
- Financial and Medicare funding review
- Accreditation and compliance review
- Survey and trend reporting
- Faculty and resident support review

## Quarterly
- Institutional risk audit
- Strategic initiative review
- Growth and recruitment review
- Trend analysis across programs
- Leadership review of operational capacity and gaps

## Operating Standard
The office should run on a defined cadence rather than reacting only when issues arise. Rhythm creates accountability, visibility, and early warning capability.
