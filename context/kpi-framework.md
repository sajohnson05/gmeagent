# KPI Framework

## Purpose
Defines key metrics used to monitor performance across the GME office.

---

## Accreditation

- Compliance status
- Site visit readiness
- Survey results
- Open risk items

---

## Finance & Medicare

- FTE counts
- Cap utilization
- DGME/IME projections
- Funding variance

---

## Resident Affairs

- Onboarding completion rate
- Open issues
- Leave compliance
- Resident satisfaction

---

## Faculty Development

- Training completion rates
- Evaluation quality metrics
- Scholarly activity participation

---

## Data & Analytics

- Dashboard usage
- Data accuracy
- Reporting timeliness

---

## Strategy & Growth

- Program pipeline
- Fill rates
- Recruitment success
- Expansion ROI

---

## Cadence

- Daily: key alerts and dashboards
- Weekly: trend review
- Monthly: performance review
- Quarterly: strategic review
