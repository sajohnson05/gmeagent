# Staffing Model

## Purpose
Defines the full staffing structure required for a complete GME office.

---

## Leadership

- DIO
- Associate DIO
- Director of GME

---

## Accreditation

- Director of Accreditation
- Accreditation Manager
- Policy Specialist
- Institutional Coordinator

---

## Finance & Medicare

- Director of Finance
- Medicare Analyst
- Budget Analyst
- Agreements Specialist

---

## Resident Affairs

- Director of Resident Affairs
- Resident Manager
- Visa Specialist
- Wellness Lead

---

## Faculty Development

- Director of Faculty Development
- Program Manager
- Documentation Specialist

---

## Data & Systems

- Director of Data
- Data Analyst
- Systems Administrator

---

## Strategy & Growth

- Director of Strategy
- Recruitment Manager
- Marketing Specialist

---

## Design Standard

- Do not combine roles early
- Avoid single points of failure
- Ensure redundancy in critical areas
