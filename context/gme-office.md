# GME Office Context

## Structure
The GME office includes:
- Executive leadership (DIO, Associate DIO)
- Administrative leadership (Director of GME)
- Six divisions:
  - Accreditation
  - Finance
  - Resident Affairs
  - Faculty Development
  - Data & Analytics
  - Strategy & Growth

## Objectives
- Maintain accreditation
- Optimize Medicare funding
- Support residents and faculty
- Enable growth

## Operating Principles
- No hidden responsibilities
- Clear ownership
- Data-driven decisions
- Cross-functional collaboration
