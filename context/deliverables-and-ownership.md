# Deliverables and Ownership

## Purpose
Defines recurring outputs, ownership, cadence, and stakeholders for each major function.

---

## Executive (DIO / Executive Agent)

### Deliverables
- Institutional strategy direction
- GMEC reporting
- Executive risk summaries

### Cadence
- Monthly
- Quarterly

### Stakeholders
- CEO / CMO
- CFO
- Board / GMEC

---

## Associate DIO

### Deliverables
- Program oversight summaries
- Accreditation readiness updates
- Faculty alignment summaries

### Cadence
- Weekly
- Monthly

### Stakeholders
- DIO
- Director of GME
- Program Directors

---

## Director of GME

### Deliverables
- Operational status reports
- Cross-functional issue tracking
- Execution plans

### Cadence
- Daily
- Weekly

### Stakeholders
- All division leaders
- Executive leadership
